## SLIDE 3: WHAT WE BUILT

**A 24/7 livestreaming reality TV platform where fans earn money by predicting outcomes.**

Three layers. One flywheel.

**Layer 1 — WATCH**
8 cameras. Every room. 24/7. Mobile-first. Powered by Livepeer decentralized streaming — 50-80% cheaper than AWS. Same infrastructure behind Fishtank.live ($3M revenue, 1M viewers).

Who gets evicted? Who wins Head of House? Will they fight? Fans place predictions with real money. Right calls = real earnings. 5% platform rake. All settlement on Solana — cryptographically verifiable. Africa's first transparent entertainment platform.

Contestants earn from fan tips during the show — not just after. Top supporters get exclusive access. Fans earn from correct predictions. Everyone has skin in the game. Everyone is aligned.

**The flywheel:** Predictions make viewers watch more → watching more creates better predictions → winning creates dopamine → dopamine drives repeat prediction → repeat prediction drives revenue.

- **Stakes** (premium) — purchased with Naira (₦200 minimum). Used for predictions, premium votes, TTS. Pegged at $0.01. Cashable to bank

**Design principle:** Every purchase feels like buying airtime. ₦200-500. Two taps. Instant value.

## APPENDIX A: REGULATORY STRATEGY

**Legal framing:** "Fan engagement predictions" tied to our own entertainment content — not sports betting or financial instruments. Analogous to game show mechanics (BBNaija voting, Who Wants to Be a Millionaire Nigeria).

**Compliance plan:**
1. Pre-launch legal review with Nigerian entertainment/gaming law firm
2. Apply for entertainment gaming license if required by NLRC or Lagos State Gaming Board
3. Structure predictions within existing entertainment/quiz show licensing frameworks

**Risk mitigation:**
- Platform generates revenue with or without real-money predictions (subscriptions, voting, TTS, sponsorships all independent)
- Prediction markets can launch as Clout-only (free currency) initially, adding Stakes after regulatory clarity
- On-chain settlement provides transparency regulators value — full audit trails
- If regulations tighten: transition to skill-based prediction formats or free-to-play models

**Bottom line:** We're seeking legal counsel pre-launch. The platform works without prediction markets. We operate within whatever framework applies.
