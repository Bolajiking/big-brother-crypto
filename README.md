# Star Factor

## Staked entertainment for African audiences.

**Watch. Predict. Earn.**

Star Factor is live reality TV where the audience has skin in the game.

Fans watch the house live, call what happens next, back the cast they believe in, and earn when their read is right. Contestants earn while the cameras are rolling. Brands get moments people actually care about.

You watched all night. You ran the campaigns. You built the stars. Star Factor is what happens when fans get a cut.

## The Deal

Reality TV is already interactive. Fans vote, post, argue, campaign, clip, defend, drag, and turn strangers into stars.

But the old model is one-way. The audience creates the heat. Networks, telcos, and advertisers capture the value.

Star Factor changes the deal.

The show is built so attention has upside. If you see the moment before everyone else, your take can be worth something. If you back the right cast member, your support shows up in the house. If a brand owns a challenge, room, or eviction moment, it owns live behavior, not just an ad slot.

Same drama. Different deal.

## Staked Entertainment

Staked entertainment is content where the people watching have something at stake.

For Star Factor, that means viewers are not just voting after the episode or reacting from the timeline. They are watching live, backing outcomes, supporting cast, and earning from correct reads while the show is still happening.

It is entertainment with receipts: the audience sees the moment, calls the moment, and shares in the value created by the moment.

## What You Do

### Watch

One Lagos house. Sixteen housemates. Every key room live.

Move from the living room to the kitchen, pool deck, diary room, garden, and main stage without waiting for an edited episode.

No edit. No cut. No spin.

### Predict

Call the moments fans already argue about:

- Who gets evicted this week?
- Who wins tonight's task?
- Will a kiss happen today?
- Which housemate starts the next fight?

Predictions live inside the viewing experience, so the show, chat, cast, and markets all move together.

### Earn

When your take lands, your balance updates. Cash out, hold it, or back the next moment.

It should feel as easy as watching with friends, buying airtime, and knowing you were right before the room caught up.

## Why It Works

Star Factor brings two existing behaviors into one product.

Millions of Nigerians already watch reality TV together. The same generation already understands prediction, odds, stakes, and fast payouts. Until now, those behaviors lived in different places.

Star Factor puts them in the same room.

That is why Star Factor starts with staked entertainment: live content where viewers, cast, brands, and the platform all have skin in the game.

## Why Live Matters

Prediction only works when people trust the moment.

A market on a taped show is fragile because someone already knows what happened. Star Factor is live, multi-camera, and built around events the audience can see. The kiss, task, fight, alliance, save, and eviction happen in front of the same people who called them.

The camera is the receipt.

## Built For

### Fans

For the people who know who is moving funny before the edit catches up.

Watch the house. Predict the drama. Earn from your takes.

### Cast

Traditional reality TV pays you in fame after the fact, and only if you make it far enough. Star Factor is built so cast members can earn during the show through fan support, challenges, tips, and prize pools.

BBNaija makes you famous after. Star Factor pays you while.

### Brands

Star Factor gives brands the energy of live reality TV with clearer engagement data and native moments to own: rooms, challenges, evictions, cast arcs, and fan campaigns.

Sponsor the room. Own the moment.

### Partners

Star Factor is the first platform for staked entertainment in African media: owned live content, native prediction behavior, direct fan participation, and visible settlement in one product loop.

## Development

This repository contains the Star Factor web app built with Next.js, React, Prisma, Privy, Socket.IO, and Livepeer-ready streaming surfaces.

### Run Locally

```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000).

For the custom Socket.IO server:

```bash
npm run dev-server
```

### Useful Commands

```bash
npm run build
npm run lint
npm run db:generate
npm run db:push
npm run db:seed
```
