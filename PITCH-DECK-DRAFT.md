## SLIDE 4: THE SOLUTION

**A 24/7 livestreaming reality TV platform where viewers earn money by predicting outcomes, supporting their favorite stars, and becoming true stakeholders.**

**Three Pillars:**

**1. Live 24/7 Streaming**
Multi-camera feeds (8 cameras) powered by Livepeer decentralized infrastructure. Watch any room, anytime. Mobile-first. Costs 50-80% less than traditional broadcast streaming.

**2. Prediction Markets**
Bet on evictions, challenges, relationships, drama. Right predictions = real earnings. We take a 5% fee on all prediction volume. On-chain settlement via Solana for full transparency.

**3. Creator Revenue Share**
Contestants earn from fan tips and engagement during the show — not just after. Top supporters get exclusive access. This attracts better talent and creates aligned incentives between fans and stars.

**Not just entertainment. A participation economy.**

## SLIDE 5: HOW IT WORKS

**Four steps. Two currencies. One loop.**

| Step | Action | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sign Up in Seconds | Email, Google, or WhatsApp. No wallet setup needed (Privy handles Web3 onboarding invisibly) |
| 2 | Watch the Action | Live 24/7 streams across 8 cameras. Pick your room. Mobile-first |
| 3 | Predict & Play | Who gets evicted? Who wins HoH? Will they fight? Place predictions, send TTS messages, vote |
| 4 | Earn Real Money | Cash out to your bank account via Paystack. Or withdraw USDC to your wallet |

**Dual Currency System:**

| Currency | How You Get It | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Clout** (Free) | Daily login (+50), watching (+10/hr), chatting (+1/msg), referrals (+500) | Basic voting, free prediction markets, chat emojis, fan badges |
| **Stakes** (Premium) | Purchase with Naira (₦200-₦100K+), or win from predictions | Real-money predictions, premium votes (1.5-3x multiplier), TTS messages, contestant tips, cash out |

**Key design:** Clout creates habit. Stakes create revenue. Free users generate attention and social buzz (they're the audience sponsors pay for). Paid users generate revenue. Both are valuable.

## APPENDIX SLIDES (If Asked)

### A1: Regulatory Strategy

**Our approach to Nigerian prediction market regulation:**

| Dimension | Our Position |
|---|---|
| **Legal framing** | "Fan predictions" — entertainment-based outcome predictions tied to our own content, not sports betting or financial instruments |
| **Regulatory body** | National Lottery Regulatory Commission (NLRC) governs gaming. Lagos State Gaming Board for Lagos-specific operations |
| **Our strategy** | Pre-launch legal review with Nigerian gaming law firm. Apply for entertainment gaming license if required. Structure prediction markets as "fan engagement" with prize elements, not wagering |
| **Precedent** | Nigerian quiz shows and game shows (Who Wants to Be a Millionaire Nigeria, BBNaija voting itself) operate under entertainment licensing, not gambling |
| **Risk mitigation** | Prediction markets can be launched as Clout-only (free currency) initially, adding Stakes predictions after regulatory clarity. Platform functions without prediction markets (subscriptions, voting, TTS still generate revenue) |
| **Key assumption** | We operate within existing entertainment licensing frameworks. If regulation tightens, we transition to skill-based prediction formats or free-to-play models |

**Bottom line:** We're seeking legal counsel pre-launch and will operate within whatever framework applies. The platform generates revenue with or without real-money predictions — it's a feature, not the entire business.
